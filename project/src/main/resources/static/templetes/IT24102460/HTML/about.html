Our Journey

1985

Foundation

SLRail Express was founded with a single route connecting downtown to the northern suburbs, serving just 2,000 passengers daily.

1995

Expansion

Expanded service to the southern region and introduced our first express trains, reducing travel time by 30%.

2005

Modernization

Launched our digital ticketing system and upgraded our entire fleet with modern, energy-efficient trains.

2015

Sustainability Focus

Committed to carbon neutrality by 2030 and began transitioning to 100% renewable energy for all operations.

2023

Innovation Leadership

Introduced AI-powered scheduling and launched our mobile app with real-time tracking and personalized journey planning.
